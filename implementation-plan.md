## 1. Landing Page Redesign (APOB.ai Style)

- Left-aligned headline "Create your AI Influencer"

## 2. Video Features (from YouTube tutorial)
- **Create AI Model**: Define age, build, hairstyle or train from reference image
- **Image Generation**: Text prompts with facial consistency
- **Image to Video**: Animate static images with text prompts (actions/exercises)
- **Edit Motion**: Motion capture from reference video to AI influencer
- **Edit Character**: Replace person in video with AI influencer
- **Face Swap**: Swap faces in videos
- **Style Editing**: Change visual style of videos
- **Background Editing**: Change video backgrounds
- **Output Settings**: Aspect ratio, resolution (up to FHD), duration
- **Pre-set Templates**: Clothing, environment, weather
